(Music) Max Winter / SkiiFall / Toro Y Moi

Max Winter's Lean Into Me. A lowkey bopper, a favorite of my super religious friend who only listens to musicals and Christian music. Lowkey, catchy vocals and piano sections, play it in front of a loved one. They’ll probably give you a kiss on the cheek right after. SkiiFall's short mixtape WOIIYOIE TAPES Vol. 1. Both Lost Angeles and My Gully have smooth harmonious hooks. Listen to Lost Angeles if you’re on your dark melancholy lone wolf shit. My Gully for a more hopeful dreamy attitude. Finally, Toro Y Moi the goat. The grounded Ye of the “underground”, your favorite’s favorite. Inner Peace is great and his most approachable album. Funky and energetic personal favorites include Who I Am and Laws of the Universe.

(Stylin' on em') Nike SB Bruin

Put on by Kunichi Nomura. If you like something simple/classy like Vans or infamous German trainers looking shoes (but not ugly, maybe I’ll come around one day. I didn’t like E36’s when I was a younger either.) look no further than the Nike SB Bruin Classy, worn by Michael J. Fox himself in Back To The Future (though these are the SB variants ). All the good color ways came out around 2010. So no you can’t find them in stores. Figure it out, I believe in you.

(Current Book) Gravity's Rainbow

Currently reading Thomas Pynchon's world renowned Gravitys Rainbow. One of the great American writers, at least that’s what I've heard. If you want to see how fragile our understanding of the world is whether through traditional or alternative systems. Say goodbye to horror films, this stuff will make you shit bricks. Start with The Crying Lot of 49 so you know what you’re getting yourself into.
